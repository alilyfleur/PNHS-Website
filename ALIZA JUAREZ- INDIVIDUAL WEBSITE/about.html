HISTORY OF THE SCHOOL

In 1965, Paulba Barangay High School was opened through the initiative of some people who were determined to make real the dream of the people in the community to have a high school that would affect a change for the better in their economic, social and educational life.

These people were Mr. Nery Camba, Principal of Paulba Elementary School; Mr. Fransisco Palma, Barangay Captain; Mr. Andres Mesina, Barangay Treasurer and Mr. Juan Pamorada, Barangay Secretary. They worked even up to the wee hours mobilizing key figures in the government and private sector to support their cause. Thus, finakky, the school was born amidst the rejoicing of the people especially the parents who could not afford to send their children to high school in the towns of Ligao, Guinobatan or Polangui.

SCHOOL PROFILE

Paulba National High School is a Public Secondary School is situated at the so-called urban Barangay of Ligao City - Barangay Paulba, Opened in 1965 as Paulba Barangay High School and nationalized in 1988-1989, it is the first public secondary school in Ligao. Since then, it has grown, developed and continously catered to students/ learners not only from barangay Paulba but also from neighboring barangays including those from Oas such as San Jose and San Pascual.

With its mission to deliver quality education to the youth, the school is on its quest for achieving its vision - a high performing school.
